Privacy-friendly summary

Your game progress is stored locally.

QuickMind Games does not require an account and does not operate its own backend server. Progress, records, statistics, preferences, and settings are stored locally on the user's device.

The app uses Google AdMob for ads, Apple Game Center for rankings, Apple StoreKit for purchases, and optional local notifications for reminders.
